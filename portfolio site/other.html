For this project I took the first paragraph from Lewis Carroll's “Alice's Adventures in Wonderland,” and trasnformed into a one line story with a more ambigious character. I worked to find the correct balance of contrast between the words and the image without “torturing the type.”

For this project I chose to incorporate my design from a previous project. I utilized the gaps that were created by groups of lines, by placing text content be- tween them. I wanted to highlight the names by placing them in the gaps created by two shorter lines, bordered on the top and bottom by two longer lines.

While creating this conceptual piece I was drawn to the tension that is observed when vertical lines are fitted closely together enough that the negative space would become a shape of its own.
